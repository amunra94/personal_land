Sales prediction

Prediction of Samsung devices sales

Collecting data - DEVELOPING - DEPLOY

This project consisted in improving the quality of the existing algorithm for predicting the sales of Samsung smartphones throughout Russia. As a nonlinear, there was a very good algorithm that worked in manual mode, with seasonal coefficients and with a quality of 72%, there was a special developed metric of the forecast quality taking into account the price of the device throughout the market. The main difficulty was that we only had data for 4 years with a weekly timeframe, and during these years some models went out of sales. Since, in addition to predicting the total sales, it was necessary to predict the sales of individual devices, which are not static. Our first algorithms were ARIMA and FBProphet, of the two the best quality was shown by the profit with the addition of additional regressors in addition to the sales themselves, but good quality was only in total sales. Now it was necessary to solve the problem of forecasting 650 time series at the same time. As a model, we used gradient boosting, with the addition of a large number of features, including look backs and training with a small learning rate, which gave us an overall increase in quality of + 5%.

 

Category prediction

Predict category for search engine

Collecting data - DEVELOPING - DEPLOY

Global Aliexpress contains about 150 million goods, where descriptions and attributes of goods have been translated from Chinese into English and other languages, which causes big problems for the search engine to find relevant goods. For this purpose, as an additional feature in the search engine and in the ranking, a model for predicting the products category by query. Which limited the search to specific categories and helped rank more relevant products higher. We used about 500 features in which there were paired features request-category of both behavior and semantics, which gave an increase in search relevance by 8%.

 

Prediction Bitcoin price

Make auxiliary trading tool

Collecting data - DEVELOPING - DEPLOY

It was a freelance project where the customer needed to develop a system in which the algorithm would predict price movement one day ahead and inform him about the forecast and sharp jumps in volume. For this purpose, two models were applied, one of which determined the mood of the news. For it I developed a parsing of news about cryptocurrency from various sources, the main one of which was Twitter. Another basic model was LSTM NN using time features of price, volume, orders and also news over time. To determine the quality and suitability of the model, a 10-day test was performed, the model had to predict 8 days correctly. As a result, it was possible to fulfill the required quality, and the model was poured on the VDS server, and through the bot telegrams it was easily configured and notified about changes and forecasts.
